Think of this as purity, starting from scratch. Remember that this occurs in context: the Second Day of Pesach where we have already left Egypt and have been liberated. The crucial first step of casting off our slavery is to get down to basics, the absolute core of everything, which is unconditional overflowing love. Generosity without restraint or qualification or reservation.

Questions for meditation: do I place conditions on love? Do I only love those who love me first? Do I only express generosity towards those that can reciprocate or do me a favor in return? Do I show love only when it's comfortable and safe?
