As we come to the end of the week, the final aspects (today of Yesod and tomorrow of Malchut) are critical from a practical sense. How can we make these more theoretical lessons learned through meditation and contemplation of the higher concepts real and actualized in our lives?

Compassion is incredibly powerful when expressed in relationship with one other person. Focus today on a single, long-term relationship that you have with another and find ways to make compassion — connectedness — the foundation of that bond.
