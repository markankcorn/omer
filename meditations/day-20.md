As we come to the end of this week of Compassion, the final finishing aspects (today of Yesod and tomorrow of Malchut) are critical from a practical sense. Yesod is the foundation, the concrete, stable base that we build on. We've spent a week dreaming and ideating, planning and experimenting. Now, how can we make these lessons learned through meditation and contemplation of the higher concepts real and actualized in our lives?

Compassion is incredibly powerful when expressed in relationship with one other person. Focus today on a single, long-term relationship that you have with another and find ways to make compassion — connectedness — the foundation of that bond. What concrete actions can you take to show this person the depths of your care and compassion and kindness?
